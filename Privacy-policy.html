Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Clic'N Clean ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our cleaning services.

By submitting a quote request or booking a service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a quote request form on our website
  • Book a cleaning appointment
  • Exchange contact information via our digital business card
  • Contact us by phone, email, or SMS

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, service address, and service preferences.

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

By providing your phone number and submitting our quote request form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Clic'N Clean.

SMS messages we send may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Booking confirmations and updates
  • Payment notifications and receipts
  • Job completion confirmations
  • Follow-up messages and rebooking reminders
  • Promotional offers (only with your explicit consent)

Message frequency: Typically 2–5 messages per booking.

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time to unsubscribe.

To get help: Reply HELP or contact us at cleaning@httservicepros.com.

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your cleaning service bookings
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations via SMS and email
  • Process payments for services rendered
  • Communicate with you about your service requests
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations (payment processors, scheduling software)
  • Cleaning contractors assigned to your job (name and address only)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by clicking unsubscribe

8. Age Restriction

Our services and SMS communications are intended solely for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. By submitting a quote request form or opting in to receive SMS messages, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that a person under 18 has submitted their information, we will delete it immediately.

Clic'N Clean is not liable for delayed or undelivered SMS messages. Message delivery is subject to effective transmission from your mobile carrier. Mobile carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message and data rates may apply based on your carrier plan.

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

What are cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional cookies — Remember your preferences to improve your experience

Third-party tracking: We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics. These services may collect information about your visits using their own cookies.

How to control cookies: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

By continuing to use our website you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
